Codecademy Project

This page is intended to fulfill the requirements of the first major Codecademy project in the full stack development course program. Please note, while the project's requirements were minimal, I chose to take it a step further and create a more elaborate page. I initially started with GIMP to create the logo seen in the top left corner before moving on to Figma to create a page design draft. The figma design can be found by clicking HERE.
